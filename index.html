Admissions 2026

A simple, transparent process designed to welcome your child into our community.

1

Submit Inquiry

Fill out our online inquiry form or visit the school office to express your interest in enrollment for the upcoming academic year.

2

Fill Admission Form

Complete the official admission form with all required documents including previous academic records and identification.

3

Interview / Assessment

A brief interview and grade-appropriate assessment helps us understand the student's strengths and learning needs.

4

Confirmation & Enrollment

Upon selection, receive your official offer letter and complete the enrollment process to secure your child's place.
